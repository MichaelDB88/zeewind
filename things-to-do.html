A holiday home in the heart of Bredene

Whether you are travelling with children, planning a relaxed coastal break, or looking for easy activities close to your holiday home in Bredene, Holiday Home Zeebries puts you close to many of the area's most practical and enjoyable options.

From swimming and family fun to a cultural day trip in Ostend, a meal inside the holiday park or a slow morning on the dunes — this guide covers the easiest things to do near Holiday Home Zeebries without needing to plan a complicated itinerary.

What to do near your holiday home

🚶

Within walking distance

Step outside and you are already there — no car needed.

Beach & Nature

Beach and dunes

Bredene is known for its natural beach and wide dunes — perfect for slow mornings, long walks, sandcastles and fresh sea air. Beach time can easily become part of your daily rhythm without needing a car or a plan.

  • Best for: All ages, walks, relaxing, families
  • Distance: Short walk from the front door
Swimming & Family Fun

LAGO Bredene Grasduinen

A modern swimming and recreation centre just a short walk away, with children's water play, indoor play area, outdoor pools, sauna and bistro. A practical choice on a rainy day or when children want a change from the beach.

  • Best for: Families, children, rainy days, active afternoons
  • Location: Oscar Goethalsstraat 4, 8450 Bredene
  • Tip: Check opening hours before visiting

Outdoor Play & Nature

Recreatiedomein Grasduinen

A nine-hectare outdoor recreation domain right on your doorstep — perfect for families, cyclists and nature lovers. Children can burn off energy on the playground or the sports fields, while adults enjoy the mountain bike circuit, walking paths or a quiet moment by the pond. Free showers, toilets and covered bike storage are all on site.

  • Best for: Families, cyclists, walkers, nature lovers, all ages
  • What's there: Playground, mountain bike trail, cycling & walking paths, pond, sports fields, fitness trail
  • Tip: Free showers and covered bike storage on site

A short ride away

Cycling & Walking

Cycling the coast

The Belgian coast is easy to explore by bike. Ride through the dunes, follow the coastal route, or cycle to a neighbouring seaside town at your own pace. A great way to see more of the area without a fixed plan.

  • Best for: Families, active guests, all ages
  • Tip: Bike rental available in Bredene and Ostend
History & Culture

Fort Napoleon

A historic coastal fort on the Oosteroever in Ostend. Follow the story trail, explore its atmospheric rooms and discover changing exhibitions. A good half-day for both adults and children, especially on cloudier days.

  • Best for: History, culture, families, rainy days
  • Address: Vuurtorenweg 13, 8400 Ostend
  • Tip: Booking in advance is recommended
Visit Fort Napoleon →
Day Trip

Ostend

A full city's worth of things to do, just minutes away. Explore the seafront, marina, museums, shops and restaurants. Works well for a few hours or a full day, and easy to combine with a visit to Fort Napoleon.

  • Best for: All ages, culture, food, rainy days
  • How to get there: Short drive or bike ride along the coast
Coastal Town

De Haan

A charming seaside town known for its Belle Époque villas and quiet coastal feel. A good option for a change of scenery — a walk, a drink, a meal, or just a slower afternoon before heading back.

  • Best for: Leisurely outings, couples, families
  • How to get there: Easy drive or coastal bike route
