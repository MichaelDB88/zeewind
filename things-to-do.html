Swimming & Family Fun

LAGO Bredene Grasduinen

One of the easiest family activities near Zeewind II. A modern swimming and recreation centre with children's water play, indoor play area, outdoor pools, sauna and bistro. A practical choice on a rainy day or when children want a change from the beach.

  • Best for: Families, children, rainy days, active afternoons
  • Location: Oscar Goethalsstraat 4, 8450 Bredene
  • Tip: Check opening hours before visiting

Historic Day Trip

Fort Napoleon in Ostend

A historic coastal landmark on the Oosteroever in Ostend, within easy reach of Bredene. Follow the story trail through the fort, explore its atmospheric rooms and discover changing exhibitions. A good half-day excursion for both adults and children.

  • Best for: History, culture, families, rainy days
  • Address: Vuurtorenweg 13, 8400 Oostende
  • Tip: Booking tickets in advance is recommended
Visit Fort Napoleon →
Beach & Nature

Beach and dunes

Bredene is known for its natural beach and dunes — perfect for slow mornings, long walks, sandcastles and fresh sea air. From Zeewind II, beach time can easily become part of your daily rhythm without needing a car or a plan.

  • Best for: All ages, walks, relaxing, families
  • Distance: Short walk from Zeewind II

Cycling and walking

The Belgian coast is easy to explore by bike or on foot. From Zeewind II, guests can enjoy routes through Bredene, the dunes and nearby coastal towns at their own pace — a short family walk, a longer bike ride, or a scenic route towards the sea.

  • Best for: Families, active guests, all ages

Day Trip

Ostend day trip

Ostend is close enough for a relaxed day trip from Bredene. Combine Fort Napoleon with the Oosteroever seafront, shops, restaurants, museums, or a coastal walk. Works well for a few hours or a full day — with or without sunshine.

  • Best for: All ages, culture, food, rainy days
  • Distance: A short drive or bike ride from Zeewind II

Nearby Seaside Town

De Haan and nearby towns

De Haan is known for its charming coastal atmosphere and Belle Époque villas. A good option when you want a change of scenery while keeping the day simple. Enjoy a walk, a drink, a meal, or just the different coastal feeling before returning to Zeewind II.

  • Best for: Leisurely outings, couples, families
  • Distance: Easy drive along the coast
Food & Drinks

Brasserie Duinenhoeve

Located right inside Holiday Park Zeewind II, next to the swimming pool. One of the most convenient places for a meal or drink without leaving the park. Child-friendly with an indoor play corner, playground nearby, and free parking. Monthly menus, à la carte and summer treats.

  • Best for: Easy meals, families, after swimming
  • Location: Inside Zeewind II, next to the pool
  • Tip: Child-friendly, indoor play corner, free parking
