Historic Day Trip

Fort Napoleon in Ostend

Fort Napoleon is a historic coastal landmark on the Oosteroever in Ostend, within easy reach of Zeewind II Holiday Park in Bredene. It is a good choice when you want to add a cultural outing to your beach holiday, or when the weather is less suited to a full day by the sea.

Visitors can follow the story trail through the fort, explore its thick walls and atmospheric rooms, and discover changing exhibitions throughout the year. The visit is compact enough for a half-day trip, but different enough to feel like a real excursion from Bredene.

For families, Fort Napoleon offers a nice change of pace from the beach. Children can explore a real historic building, while adults get an easy cultural stop that can be combined with a walk, lunch, or more time in Ostend. It is also a useful option for guests who want to discover more of the Belgian coast without travelling far from their holiday home.

More ways to enjoy your stay

From Zeewind II, beach time can easily become part of your daily rhythm: a walk before breakfast, an afternoon in the sand, or an evening stroll when the coast becomes quieter.

The Belgian coast is easy to explore by bike or on foot. From Zeewind II, guests can enjoy local routes through Bredene, the dunes and nearby coastal towns.
