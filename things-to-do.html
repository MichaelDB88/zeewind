New in Bredene

LAGO Bredene Grasduinen

LAGO Bredene Grasduinen is the new sport and recreation centre in Bredene. It is a strong option for families who want a swimming day nearby, especially when the weather turns or children need a change from the beach.

According to LAGO, the centre includes a swimming paradise with an interactive children's pool, a wild-water attraction, outdoor pools, a sauna, a play creek, a bistro, fitness options, and an indoor play area.

  • Best for Families, children, swimming, rainy days
  • Address Oscar Goethalsstraat 4, 8450 Bredene
  • Useful to know Check opening hours before you go
  • Tickets Available through the official LAGO website
